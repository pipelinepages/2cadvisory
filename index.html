About 2C Legal

Built for founders who move fast — and still need to get it right.

2C Legal was started because too many founders were learning legal lessons the expensive way: after a bad contract, a missed compliance step, or a dispute over who actually owns the code. We work as an embedded legal partner for startups and technology companies, translating dense legal requirements into clear decisions you can act on.

Our team combines traditional corporate and commercial law with deep familiarity in technology, data, and intellectual property — including trademark and patent protection, and the software licensing agreements that determine how your product can be sold, white-labeled, or integrated by others. We pair that expertise with our AI Legal Assessment tool, so every engagement starts with a clear picture of where you stand.
